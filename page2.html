Information Systems Concepts (ISCG5424)

Level: 5
Credits: 15 (0.125 EFTS)
Compulsory: Yes
Aim: To provide students with an introduction to information systems and the role they play within industry. The main purpose of the course is to enable a student to engage with a range of information system elements and to make choices for future specialist study.
Pre-requisites / Co-requisites: Students must be admitted to either the Bachelor of Computing Systems or the Diploma in Information Technology Support or the Graduate Diploma in Computing to enrol in this course. Restriction: ISCG5410. Pre-requisite for DITS: ISCG4500.
Restriction: ISCG5236.
Topics include: Systems theory, stages in the SDLC, data flow diagrams, activity diagrams, requirement elicitation techniques, selected modelling tools Other topics as negotiated
